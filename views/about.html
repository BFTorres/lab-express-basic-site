About Baby Yoda

Grogu is a member of the same alien species as the Star Wars character Yoda.[6][7] He is 50 years old during the events of The Mandalorian, but still appears to be an infant because of the pace at which that species matures.[8][9][10] The species has never been given a proper name because Star Wars creator George Lucas wanted Yoda to maintain a sense of mystery.[2][4][11] Until Chapter 13 (i.e. episode 5 of season 2) of The Mandalorian, Grogu was not identified by a proper name, being referred to by sympathetic characters as "the child", "the kid", or "the baby", and by the antagonists as "the asset", "the bounty", "the target", or "the donor".
